Our Story

We are currently a two person software company operating with the power of 200. Flyntlok is also an homage to our favorite cartoon character, the inventor of the Flint Lockwood Diatonic Super Mutating Dynamic Food Replicator or FLDSMDFR food machine and, our personal favorite, spray on shoes. He was cool like us.
